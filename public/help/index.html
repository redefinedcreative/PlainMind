Getting started

How do I save something to PlainMind?

In any app — Safari, Music, Maps, Photos, and more — tap the share button, then tap PlainMind. The item lands in your library, organized by what it is. No tagging or folders required.

What kinds of things can I save?

Links, music and albums, podcasts, places, photos and screenshots, PDFs and documents, posts, videos, App Store apps, and plain text. PlainMind detects the type for you.

Organizing & search

How is my library organized?

Automatically, by content type. Filter by Links, Music, Images, Places, PDFs, or Text, and use Quick Searches to surface what you saved this week, this month, or items you've saved but never opened.

Can I search inside PDFs and images?

Yes. Search reaches inside titles, descriptions, text recognized in images, and full PDF contents — all on your device.

Privacy & sync

Where is my data stored?

On your device by default. PlainMind is local-first, with no tracking and no feed. With a Pro plan you can turn on optional iCloud sync, which keeps your library in your own iCloud account.

Can I export my library?

Yes. PlainMind exports a self-contained backup bundle that includes your metadata and saved files. Your library is your file — not locked behind an account.

Plans & billing

What does it cost?

Free includes core capture, basic search, and local storage. Pro is $1.99/month or $19.99/year and adds iCloud sync, full search, and collections. A Max tier with AI features follows after launch.

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled through the App Store. Manage or cancel any time from Settings on your iPhone under your Apple Account.
