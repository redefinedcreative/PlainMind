How it works

  1. Save from anywhere

    From any app, in one tap through the iOS share sheet. PlainMind captures it instantly.

  2. Auto-organized by type

    Links, music, places, PDFs, images, text — sorted by what it is. No tagging, no folders.

  3. Find anything, fast

    On-device search reaches inside titles, text, and PDFs to surface what you remember.

  4. Private, local-first

    Everything stays on your device by default. Your library is yours alone.
